A one-day bootcamp on mutual **influence**, modern **leadership** and accurate **management** in highly dynamic and complex organisations. Co-facilitated with [Andrea Provaglio](https://www.andreaprovaglio.com/), held as part of [Agile Slovenia 2025](https://www.agileslovenia.com/).

Drawing on a combined experience of over 30 years in helping complex business organisations to thrive, we explored the fundamental questions that matter in today's workplaces and practical ways to make a difference:

- How to improve the organisation's **business performance** by creating more engagement and collaboration
- How to maintain alignment with **business objectives** while creating space for autonomous decision-making, in-person and remotely
- How to promote **generational inclusion** and support greater organisational resilience and collective intelligence
- Which **technical skills** are required by managers and leaders, and to what extent they should express them
- How **AI, leadership** and **innovation** are interwoven, and how to deal with them

Although we covered leadership and organisational issues, we also focused on the single aspect around which you have the most freedom: **yourself**.

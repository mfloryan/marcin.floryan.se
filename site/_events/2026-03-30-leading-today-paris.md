A one-day, in-person workshop for middle and senior managers on staying relevant as a leader in the face of increasing uncertainty and technological complexity.

Co-facilitated with [Andrea Provaglio](https://www.andreaprovaglio.com/), hosted by [Theodo](https://www.theodo.fr/) in central Paris organised by FlowCon 2026 conference.

Topics covered:

- Management, Leadership and AI
- Distributed work and remote collaboration
- Organisational alignment while enabling autonomy
- Manager competencies in AI governance
- Responsible AI from a management perspective

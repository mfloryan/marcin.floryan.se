On Tuesday I have had a very interesting evening. It was time for the [ALE Bathtub IV](https://www.bathtubconferences.org/bathtub/final-agenda-for-bathtub-iv-the-xmas-edition/) conference - Christmas Edition, the informal, webinar-style event organised by the [ALE network](https://alenetwork.eu/) as yet another way to share ideas and grow the network. [Catia](https://plus.google.com/111012996835051199986), who lead the organisational effort this time, invited me to contribute and, with some trepidation, I agreed.

I'm glad I did.

Despite some technical problems with citrix I've managed to share my ideas with others and they seem to have been very well received. Thank you very much for listening.
